Dear Ashmita,

In a world of noise, you are my quiet song,

A gentle light where all my thoughts belong.

It’s in the quiet way you speak, the warmth you share,

A simple truth that shows how much I care.


No grandeur needed, no dramatic play,

Just wishing to brighten each and every day.

I watch you chase your dreams, so brave and free,

And hope that you find joy in all you see.


For words are fleeting, but the actions stand,

A quiet promise, like writing in the sand.

I'm here to support you, to listen, to care,

Whenever you need, I'll always be there.

With sincere warmth,
